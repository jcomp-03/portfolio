About Me!

Again, welcome to my website! I started coding only in December 2021, when I enrolled in a coding bootcamp. So, this page from which you are reading this text is a proverbial work-in-progress. I decided to enroll in this boot camp for a lot of reasons, but in a nutshell, I wanted to try something new for my profession.

For a little background on who I am, I was born in Ireland, raised in Miami, Florida, and have lived in a number of different cities since moving away from home after high school. Those cities are New York, Atlanta, Albuquerque, Portland, and Lima (Peru, that is!). I have had great experiences living in those cities, each of which taught me valuable nuggets of wisdom and helped me form my world perspective. If I had to choose which city I look back on most fondly, I think I would select Portland; the Pacific Northwest is absolutely stunning and offers a balanced city-and-outdoors lifestyle.
